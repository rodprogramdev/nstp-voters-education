ONCE UPON A TIME


There was Juan who was a proud citizen of the Philippines. However, he was not that informed about his rights of suffrage. He knew that most people practice it, but he doesn’t understand its importance.


Now, as a person like Juan. We need to go on a learning journey so that we can help Juan understand the importance of our rights of suffrage. Try to answer each of the questions below and maximize the use of the website to gain points for your teams.
